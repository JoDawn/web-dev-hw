I am an art student at the University of Montana. I often hear people say, "I could never make something nice enough to hang in my home."
Well, I decided that cannot be true. I wanted to create this instructable to open the possibility to people who think they aren't creative. I wanted to show them something easy and not intimidating to create that they would enjoy and be proud of accomplishing.
